About

Engineer by background, instructor by trade, builder by habit.

I'm currently an instructor at CyberTex Institute, teaching cloud and security fundamentals to working students and career switchers. My background is in IT and infrastructure — 10+ years across networking, cloud, and systems work.

Outside of teaching, I run a small builders' community called HackersHideout (HHO). We work on hands-on projects together — some technical, some not. The goal is simple: a group of operators who learn out loud, ship things, and look out for each other.
